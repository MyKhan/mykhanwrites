## PART 1 (Action B) — Novel-page hero image prompt

**Use for:** the hero on the Novel bridge page (NOT the homepage). This is the image that carries
the book's specific world. Built from your **confirmed motifs** (rain, paper crane, little girl in a
yellow raincoat, windows/reflections); refine once the premise is final.

**Design goals**
- Evoke a *specific world*, warm but mysterious; grounded, not magical/fantasy.
- Negative space (top-left or right third) for the title + one line of text.
- Dreamlike semi-realism — **must not look AI-glossy.**
- The yellow raincoat is a *hint*, never the subject.

### Primary prompt (model-agnostic; works in Midjourney v6/v7, Flux, DALL·E 3, etc.)

```
A quiet, cinematic literary book-cover scene: looking out through a rain-streaked
window at dusk onto a near-empty street. Warm amber lamplight from inside reflects
softly on the wet glass; the world outside is rain-grey and muted. Far down the
street, small and slightly blurred by rain, a lone child in a pale-yellow raincoat
stands facing away. On the windowsill in the foreground, in soft focus, a single
folded white origami paper crane and a couple of loose handwritten pages. Mood:
warm at the center, lonely and a little mysterious at the edges — comfort with a
cold corner. Dreamlike semi-realism, painterly, film-still atmosphere, gentle grain,
natural soft light, restrained. Muted palette of charcoal, rain-grey, warm parchment
and amber, with the raincoat the only saturated note. Generous empty space in the
upper third for a title. Vertical/portrait composition.
```

**Parameters / notes**
- Aspect ratio: **portrait 4:5 or 2:3** for the prompt above (book-cover feel). Also render a
  **landscape 16:9** crop for the desktop hero and a **4:5** for mobile. (Midjourney: `--ar 2:3`.)
- Style nudge if too glossy: add "subtle film grain, slightly imperfect, hand-made feel, not
  hyper-detailed, no CGI sheen."
- Keep the crane **white/cream** (not glowing/colored) so it doesn't turn magical.

### Negative prompt (avoid)

```
no text, no lettering, no watermark, no logos; no glowing or magical crane; no cute
or cartoon style; no thriller/horror tropes, no blood, no heavy shadows; no neon, no
oversaturation; no busy clutter; no melted/warped details, no extra limbs or fingers,
no uncanny faces (keep the child small, distant, faceless); no stock-photo gloss, no
HDR, no lens-flare spam.
```

### Two variations to try

- **A — Threshold:** an open doorway/porch at dusk, rain falling beyond it, a yellow raincoat hung on
  a hook just inside, a paper crane on a side table. (Reads as "someone just arrived / left.")
- **B — Empty chairs in the rain:** two chairs on a porch or by a window in light rain, one paper
  crane on the seat, a faint yellow smudge at the far edge of frame. (Leans into "they only came
  when it rained" without showing who.)

### Homepage (author) hero — direction note (no full scene)
If you go typographic (recommended): a near-empty dark/charcoal field, one small lit window OR a
single paper crane in a corner, lots of negative space for the name + hook. Generate as a **texture/
background**, not a literal desk. (If you insist on the desk image you like: crop it to a side panel,
darken it, remove steam, retouch AI tells.)
