# 15. Summary of Current Strategy

This section can be left as-is unless the strategy changes.

MyKhanWrites.com should be a focused, elegant author hub for Meher Yar Khan.

It should make him feel like a serious literary and cinematic storyteller without trapping him in one genre.

The site should support the upcoming cozy literary fiction novel while leaving room for neo-noir, psychological thriller, speculative fiction, screenwriting, and future interactive story worlds.

The strongest current identity is:

> A writer of warm stories with cold corners — lonely people, hidden selves, and the places they go to feel less alone.

The visual identity should use:

> Warm room, dark window, lamplight, unfinished pages, subtle mug, and an origami paper crane.

The technical direction should be:

> Astro + Tailwind CSS, deployed on Hostinger, fast and mostly static, with subtle CSS animation and no unnecessary complexity.
